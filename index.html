Intresting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the Iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Intresting Facts about Camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Intresting Facts about Pandas

  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).

Intresting Facts about Teddy

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.

Intresting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes.
  • Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.
  • There are around 3,000 cicada species
